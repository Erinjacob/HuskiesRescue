If you want a husky they want to be with you. The husky is a pack animal and wants to be apart of yours. Any husky you have in your life will want to be close to you. So keep your husky inside with you. Make them a comfy place to sleep if you don’t want them on the furniture but keep your husky with you.

Exercise is absolutely necessary for a husky to have a happy life. They have been bread for generations to have the energy to pull a sled, its owner, and whatever cargo the sled contained across thousands of miles over many days in some of the harshest conditions known to man. If you do not take them out for regular walks, the husky will find something else to do with all that extra energy. Like eating your couch, shoes, homework, or anything else they fancy at the moment.

Huskys shed, they shed all the time. You will want to brush your husky for about fifteen minuets every day to keep their coat looking nice and glossy as well as reducing itching. There are two times a year where a husky will “blow their coat” and the shedding will pick up speed noticeably. Daily brushing will save you multiple vacuums.
