About Us

Husky rescue is here to help you find your Very best friend. Our mission is to unite our furry friends with Loving homes to call their own forever. We will support feed house and love as many Of our four legged Buddys as we can. But to do That we need help from people like you. we want to create a network of happy husky Owners who will work together to support Each other in the locating and caring for our Husky pals. Take a look around we hope you find your Forever home here.
